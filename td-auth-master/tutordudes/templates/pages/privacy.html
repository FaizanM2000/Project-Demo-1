We offer our users peace of mind. While surfing, users have the right to deactivate their account at any time. Please note, once this function is triggered, it will erase all account information from our systems and will immediately log you out.

All plans are billed annually, unless stated otherwise. Other than the subscription plans, there is an annual maintenance fee of $19, regardless of the current plan. All sessions are backed by our “Satisfaction Promise.”
